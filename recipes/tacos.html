Taacos

Description

tacos is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onions, and herbs (optional for seasoning)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef (or Italian sausage) until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato sauce and seasonings to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish. Repeat layers.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until bubbly and cheese is melted.
  8. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
